# Developer Checklists

## ADR
- Scope and decision boundary are explicit.
- Alternatives and consequences are documented.
- Architecture and reference links are present.

## Proposal
- Problem statement and recommendation are clear.
- Expected impact on `include/yai/` or `lib/` surfaces is identified.

## Runbook
- Preconditions and expected outputs are explicit.
- Procedure points to concrete commands under `cmd/`, `tools/`, or `tests/`.

## Milestone/Report
- Evidence bundle is attached.
- Closure rationale is explicit and traceable.
